Nice to meet you. This is me.


“Taking on a challenge is a lot like riding a horse. If you’re comfortable while you’re doing it, you’re probably doing it wrong”


- Ted Lasso

I strive on learning new things everyday. Whether it be obscure productivity tips, geeking out over space exploration, trying out electronic music production, or picking up new languages to gain an appreciation of the world and it’s different cultures. My curiosity, coupled with a strong desire to put out nothing but the best is my greatest asset. It is what drove me to pursue an Undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering.

During this time, I gained many a valuable lessons about managing teams across multiple disciplines and creating a pipeline for collective success through my time with the collegiate Formula Student Racing team. I also had the opportunity to work with an EV Startup, which gave me an appreciation for the value of sustainable engineering, not as an afterthought, but as a foundational principle. During these pursuits, my interest in the field of Aerodynamics and Computational Fluid Dynamics was increasing which I was fortunate enough to explore at my second internship, conducting research with some of the smartest people I have met.

I then decided to come to Purdue for Grad School and study Aeronautics and Astronautics to combine my love for space and further this interest, which is when I discovered the field of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, and nothing has ever been the same.
