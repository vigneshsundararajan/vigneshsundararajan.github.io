Nice to meet you. This is me.


“Taking on a challenge is a lot like riding a horse. If you’re comfortable while you’re doing it, you’re probably doing it wrong”


- Ted Lasso

I strive on learning new things everyday. Whether it be obscure productivity tips, or practicing new languages to see the sparkle in people’s eyes when you talk to them in their tongue. My curiosity, coupled with a strong desire to put out nothing but the best is my greatest asset. It is what drove me to pursue an Undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering, during which I gained many a valuable lessons about managing teams across multiple subfields and creating a pipeline for collective success in my time with the collegiate FSAE team. I also had the opportunity to work with an EV Startup, which gave me an appreciation for the value of sustainable engineering. During these pursuits, my interest in the field of Aerodynamics and Computational Fluid Dynamics was increasing which I was fortunate enough to explore at my second internship.

I then decided to come to Purdue for Grad School and study Aeronautics and Astronautics to further this interest, which is when I discovered the field of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, and nothing has ever been the same.
